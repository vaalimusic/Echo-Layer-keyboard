Echo Layer Privacy Policy

We do not collect analytics, ads identifiers, or telemetry.

The keyboard operates locally on-device.

Invisible messaging features process your text locally on the device.

AI rewriting is optional. If you enable it, the current draft text and your configured system prompt are sent only to the AI provider you selected:
- OpenAI
- OpenRouter
- Ollama
- Yandex

Echo Layer does not add its own cloud service between you and the configured provider.

Clipboard-related hidden message detection only works with text available to the keyboard or copied by the user.

If you use a remote AI provider, that provider's own privacy policy and retention rules apply to requests you send through it.
